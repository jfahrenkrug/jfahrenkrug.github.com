--- name: introducing-vertexhelper-for-box2d layout: post title: Introducing VertexHelper for Box2D & Chipmunk Vertices time: 2010-02-26 05:52:00 Z --- I found it extremely tedious to define the vertices of sprites for the Box2D or Chipmunk physics engines. Maybe I was just not using the right tool. So I wrote my own. It's called VertexHelper and it lets you graphically define vertices of sprites for Box2D and Chipmunk bodies/shapes. You can find it on github.
